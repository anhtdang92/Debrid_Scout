About Debrid Scout

Torrent search and Real-Debrid integration, with VR streaming support.

Overview

Debrid Scout is a torrent management app built with Flask and Python. It integrates with Real-Debrid's caching service to let you search torrents across indexers and stream content instantly through direct download links.

Key Features

  • Torrent Search — Search across multiple indexers via Jackett with real-time streaming results.
  • Real-Debrid Integration — Instant cache checking and unrestricted download links.
  • One-Click Streaming — Launch content directly in VLC or HereSphere VR.
  • Torrent Manager — View, browse files, and manage your RD library.
  • VR Support — Native HereSphere and DeoVR APIs with projection detection.

How It Works

Debrid Scout queries Jackett for torrent metadata, checks Real-Debrid for cached availability, and generates unrestricted download links for video files. The streaming pipeline uses Server-Sent Events to deliver results in real time as they're processed.

Community

Debrid Scout is open source and community-driven. Contributions, bug reports, and feature requests are welcome.
